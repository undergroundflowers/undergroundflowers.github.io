Body 03 / 06

The project in long form.

This project focuses on skin — our containers as human beings — and investigates it by digging deep into related topics like "Touch", "Difference" and "Change". Showing different aspects of the topic from a literal and also emotional point of view while creating empathy.

Research was grounded in first-person interviews and long-form observation. The visual language draws from tactile materials — grain, weave, porous surfaces — translated into type and grid decisions.

A sincere and intimate magazine that the reader would find a piece of themselves in — feeling understood, connected, and seen. Every page is a conversation between the reader and the body they inhabit.
